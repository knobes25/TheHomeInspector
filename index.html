    • Home Inspection Package
      Read More

      I will inspect your property from top to bottom. With a trained eye, I can not only point out present issues but also conducive conditions that can lead to costly repairs down the road. Whether your planning on selling your home and need a pre-sale inspection or a purchaser considering your dream home, rest assured that The Home Inspector can meet all your needs.

      Read More

      Getting ready to move in to your dream home? Take the stress off your shoulders with a detailed Move-In Ready inspection. I'll check for leaks, water damage, cracked or broken pipes, any structural damage, etc.

    • Infrared Inspection
      Read More

      An Infrared thermal imaging camera spots small, but key changes in temperature in different areas of a house, identifying problematic areas that the naked eye never could and some that a visual inspection could only hypothesize. An infrared inspection gives you extra peace of mind!
